What's a-happening, Hot Stuff??

April 26, 2015

Going through the process of building a two-page website as homework after 4 days of the Spring Cohort at TTS Raleigh has absolutely blown my mind. I think I understand the concepts of HTML and CSS, but putting concepts into practice has proven a bit more challenging. Don't get me wrong...I'm not complaining! As this project has come together I have managed to confuse and amaze myself. I have hit multiple dead-ends along the way and (with the help of the plethora of information available on the internet) found solutions to those problems. I know enough to realize that my coding during this project is not "pretty" or efficient. But before April 20th, none of this would have ever been possible!!
